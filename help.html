Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Yokai eSports?

Yokai eSports is a competitive gaming organization that fields teams in multiple games, creates engaging content, and collaborates with partners in the gaming industry.

2. How can I join a Yokai eSports team?

We hold tryouts and scouting events periodically. Visit our Teams page for the latest announcements and requirements.

3. Where can I find Yokai eSports news and updates?

All news, announcements, and event updates are posted on our News page and our social media channels.

4. How can I purchase Yokai eSports merchandise?

Merchandise is available in our Shop. We offer apparel, accessories, and limited-edition items.

5. How do I contact Yokai eSports?

You can reach out through our Contact Page for inquiries regarding partnerships, media, or general questions.

6. Does Yokai eSports offer sponsorships or partnerships?

Yes! We collaborate with brands and organizations. Visit our Partners page for more information.

7. Can I apply to work at Yokai eSports?

We occasionally have job openings. Check our Careers page for current opportunities.
