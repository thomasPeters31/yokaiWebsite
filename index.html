About Yokai

Born from a dream, Yokai is an esports team that embodies the mystical energy of Japanese yokai. Each member channels the spirit of these legendary beings—playful kitsune, elusive tanuki, fierce oni, and ethereal yurei—into their gameplay, turning every match into a supernatural spectacle. Founded on passion, creativity, and fearless ambition, the team reflects its creator’s journey: a young gamer who finally dared to transform her lifelong love for Japanese folklore and competitive gaming into reality. With a style as unpredictable as a mischievous yokai and teamwork as precise as a well-cast spell, Yokai isn’t just a team—it’s a living legend in the making, blending tradition, modernity, and raw talent into a force that haunts the competition.
